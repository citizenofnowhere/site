#CITIZENOFNOWHERE

I am tired.
Tired of being ashamed of my country.
Of its action and inaction,
Of its hollow opulence,
Of its indifferent privilege.

I am broken by howling
Xenophobia, fear and ignorance
Masquerading as
Patriotism, strength and common sense.

I am sickened by a system designed
To make the rich richer and the poor
Forever exploited.

By those who don't recognise
That the lot theyâ€™ve been given,
Is not merely a function of
Merit or virtue or intelligence
But in the first instance as
An accident of their birth.

By those who solve problems
By shutting boarders,
As if disease, climate change and hate
Respected them to begin with.

I am tired and
I am angry and
I yearn for a different world.

I am for the voiceless.
I am for the down-trodden.
I am for those whom society over-looks.
Whom society shuts out,
Dehumanises, vilifies and decries.
I am for the least, the last and the lost.

I am a citizen of the world.
I am a citizen of nowhere.
